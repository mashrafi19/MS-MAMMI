Our Story

About Us

Over four decades of trusted excellence in textile raw materials & accessories. M/S Mammi is a trusted name in textile raw materials and garment accessories in Bangladesh.

Established for more than 40 years, M/S Mammi is a premier supplier of textile raw materials and accessories, proudly serving both wholesale and retail clients. Built on a foundation of integrity and industry expertise, we have grown into a reliable partner for businesses that demand quality at every stage of their supply chain.

We supply a comprehensive range of products tailored to meet the demands of garment manufacturers, fashion brands and independent traders alike. Our ability to serve both large-scale wholesale orders and individual retail needs sets us apart in a competitive market.
